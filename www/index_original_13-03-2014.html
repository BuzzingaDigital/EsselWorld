    How To Reach
    • By Road
    • By Train
    • By Ferry

    How To Reach

    By Road

    • Coming by car from Mumbai, take the Western Express Highway, turn left to Kashimira and drive down to Mira-Bhayander road. Our signages will guide you to the parks. MBBT Bus no. 4 from Bhayander bus depot takes you directly to our parks.

    How To Reach

    By Train

    • Take a train on the western line to Malad or Borivali station. From Malad (w), take BEST bus no. 272 or an auto rickshaw to Marve beach. From Borivali station (w), take BEST bus no. 294 or an auto rickshaw to Gorai creek.

    How To Reach

    By Ferry

    • From Marve beach/ Gorai creek, Esselworld's own ferries are available to take you to the park.
    • Ferries are available every 15 minutes from Gorai creek and every 45 minutes from Marve beach. Ferries will be available till the last guests leaves the park.
